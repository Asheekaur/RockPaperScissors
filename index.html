game rules

object :

To play Rock, Paper, Scissors with 2 players in the game and one of them will be the first to score 5 .

set up :

Make sure that the score board shows point 0 in the beginning .

playing :

Player will click on the icon of his/her choice and computer will accept the command. When all players are ready, game will begin and score will keep on updating on the score board as game proceeds.

scoring

You beat another player if your choice "beats" theirs in the following ways :

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Each round you score 1 for every other player you beat .

ending and winning the game :

The first player to score 5

in case of a tie :

The tying players play against each other until there is one winner .
